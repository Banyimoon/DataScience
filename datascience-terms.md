## Probability Theory

**Joint probability** $p(x,y)$

**Conditional probability** $p(y|x)$

**Muttual Informaiton (MI)** $MI(x,y) = \frac{p(x,y)}{p(x)p(y)}$

**Conditional Mutual Information** $MI(A;B|C) = MI(A;B,C) - MI(A;C)$
